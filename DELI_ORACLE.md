The Pitch: "The Deli Oracle"

Tagline: He’s seen it all. He doesn’t care. But he’s got the answers.
1. The Hook (30 Seconds)
"Every New Yorker knows the real boss of the neighborhood isn't the Mayor—it’s the cat sitting on the stack of 12-pack sodas at your local bodega. They’ve witnessed 20 years of gentrification, midnight breakups, and millions of bacon-egg-and-cheeses. But until today, they weren't talking."
2. The Problem (30 Seconds)
"NYC is a city of gatekept secrets and high-stress commutes. We wanted to take the friction of city life and turn it into Play. People don't want another 'concierge' app; they want the raw, cynical, heart-of-gold perspective that makes New York, New York."
3. The Solution: "The Deli Oracle" (60 Seconds)
"We built a lean, AI-powered 'vibe-engine' that lets you talk to Stale Donut, a grizzled Brooklyn cat.
The Interface: A midnight-themed web app that looks like a neon-lit deli.
The Tech: A zero-backend Next.js build using the Vercel AI SDK to stream high-personality responses from a custom-tuned LLM.
The 'Play' Factor: Users can challenge the cat’s mood, ask for neighborhood 'tea,' or get roasted for their 'fancy' coffee orders."
4. Why It Wins (30 Seconds)
"It’s NYC-specific, it’s instantly viral, and we built it in 120 minutes with no backend. It turns the city’s grittiness into a digital playground. We aren't solving a logistics problem—we're solving a vibe problem."
